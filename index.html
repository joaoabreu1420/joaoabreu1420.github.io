about

musician, entrepreneur.

Born in Santo Tirso (Portugal), 2000. Bachelor of Music in Viola by Conservatorium van Amsterdam, The Netherlands (2018-2022). Master of Music in Viola by Conservatorium van Amsterdam, The Netherlands (2022-present). Master of Music in Viola by Haute École de Musique de Genève, Switzerland (2022-present). Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation grat holder (2022-present).

Founder - Violist at the Mankes Piano Quartet (2019-present). Violist at the Portuguese Chamber Orchestra (2021-present). Violist at EUYO - European Union Youth Orchestra (2022-present).

Founder - President of the Board at FAMART - Associação Cultural e Artística Juvenil (2021-present). Founder - Executive/Artistic & Pedagogical Director at CIMS - Cidnay International Masterclass Series - Festival & Academy (2021-present).
